Price Reduction On Bajaj Pulsar 150 Twin Disc | BikeBD

Uttara Motors Ltd has announced a price reduction on Bajaj Pulsar 150 Twin Disc in Bangladesh.Bajaj Pulsar 150 is the most sold 150 cc motorcycle
in Bangladesh & adding twin disc and some new features gave more appeal to the motorcycle among the Pulsar lovers in Bangladesh.

The original price of Bajaj Pulsar 150 Twin Disc was 1,86,900 BDT now after a price reduction of 4,000 BDT the new price of the bike will be 1,82,900 BDT. This
new price will be valid from 10th October till 31st October 2019.
